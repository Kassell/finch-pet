# Pet

A desktop pet that lives on your screen. It floats above your windows without covering the menu bar or Dock, stays out of Mission Control, and reacts to what the Agent is doing — when a background session finishes, it pops a speech bubble; double-click the bubble to jump into that session.

## How to play

- **Drag** it anywhere, across displays; fling it and it glides with inertia.
- **Click** to wave, **double-click** to jump, **right-click** to send it home (hide).
- Just tell the Agent: "switch pet", "make the pet say hi", "hide the pet".

## Changing pets

Ships with `boba` the bubble-tea otter. Compatible with [Petdex](https://petdex.dev) pet packs — hand the Agent a Petdex page URL, an image URL, or a local image / folder / zip and say "add this pet" (spritesheets are parsed as a fixed 8 × 9 grid; 192×208 per frame is the base size, higher-resolution sheets with the same grid work too).
